Sri Amarnath Yatra Tour

Destinations : Srinagar-Sonamarg-Batlal-Amarnath
Duration : 3 Nights /4 Days

Special Prices

Persons    M.A.P.
2 Persons    14500
3 Persons    14500
4 Persons    13500
5 Persons    12000

The Above rate is Per Person

All applicable hotel & transport taxes.
    Yatra Includes
  • Srinagar Airport Pickup and Drop
  • 1 Night accommodation in Deluxe Hotel in Sonamarg with breakfast & dinner on twin sharing basis
  • 2 Nights accommodation in Srinagar Hotel/ Houseboats with breakfast & dinner on twin sharing basis.
  • Neelgrath (near Baltal) Panchtarni to Neelgrath by Helicopter.(Both way)
  • Airport Transfer by car with English speaking driver.
  • Car Including: Fuel, Parking
  • Shikara Tour in Dal Lake.

- ITINERARY -

Day 1 : Jammu

Pickup from Airport / Jammu Railway Station and Transfer to Katra , and Check in Hotel, Overnight Stay in Hotel.

Day 2 : Katra

After an early breakfast start your yatra to Vaishnoo Devi Temple. After pooja & darshan start your journey back to the hotel.
Arrive at your hotel by evening. Dinner and overnight at the hotel.

Day 3 : Leave Jammu

After breakfast time free till departure. Transfer by car to Jammu airport or Railway station.

EXTENSION

Day 3 : Katra - Patinitop

After Breakfast Drive to Patnitop and Overnight Stay in Patnitop.

Day 4 : Patnitop to - Jammu

After breakfast time free till departure. Transfer by car to Jammu airport or Railway station.
