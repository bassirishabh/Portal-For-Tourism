- ITINERARY -

Day 1:Arrival Srinagar(Sightseeing)

On arrival at Srinagar, you will meet our representative at the airport and transfer to Deluxe Houseboat. Evening lake trip to 'Char Chinar' i.e. four big chinar trees standing in middle of the whole lake, 'Floating Gardens' & interesting canals by a luxurious shikara. Over night stay at houseboat.

Day 2:Pahalgam

After Breakfast drive down to Pahalgam .En-route visit Saffron fields, Anantnag Sulphur Springs. Drive through the green pine forest and along the Lidder River. On arrival, Pahalgam check-in to your Hotel. Enjoy local sightseeing of Pahalgam. Overnight stay in Hotel ( Pahalgam ).

Day 3:Pahalgam to back Srinagar Airport

After Breakfast Back to Srinagar and transfer you to Srinagar for your connecting flight to Delhi.
