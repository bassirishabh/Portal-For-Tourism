Mata Vaishno Devi Tour

Destinations : Jammu - Katra - Mata Darshan - Katra - Jammu
Duration : 2 Nights /3 Days

Special Prices

Persons    M.A.P.
2 Persons    6000
3 Persons    5500
4 Persons    5000
5 Persons    4500

The Above rate is Per Person

WE ARRANGE PICK-UP FROM JAMMU TO SRINAGAR THE COST WILL BE EXTRA
    The Package Includes
  • 2 nights stay in Srinagar Houseboat on M.A.P
  • Airport or Jammu Railway Station Pickup Transfer to Katra
  • Mata Darshan
  • Back to Jammu and Half Day City Tour
  • M.A.P Means Breakfast And Dinner

- ITINERARY -

Day 1 : Jammu

Pickup from Airport / Jammu Railway Station and Transfer to Katra , and Check in Hotel, Overnight Stay in Hotel.

Day 2 : Katra

After an early breakfast start your yatra to Vaishnoo Devi Temple. After pooja & darshan start your journey back to the hotel.
Arrive at your hotel by evening. Dinner and overnight at the hotel.

Day 3 : Leave Jammu

After breakfast time free till departure. Transfer by car to Jammu airport or Railway station.

EXTENSION

Day 3 : Katra - Patinitop

After Breakfast Drive to Patnitop and Overnight Stay in Patnitop.

Day 4 : Patnitop to - Jammu

After breakfast time free till departure. Transfer by car to Jammu airport or Railway station.
